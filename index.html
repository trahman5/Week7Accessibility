Here are some fun facts about Philadelphia:

  • #1 Fun Fact: Did you know, the University of Pennsylvania was founded by Benjamin Franklin in 1740, making it the 5th oldest college in the country?
  • #2 Fun Fact: Did you know, the author, Edgar Allen Poe lived in Philadelphia for six years and published dozens of works during his time in the city? The home he lived in is an important local landmark that is open for free tours.
  • #3 Fun Fact: Did you know, the Pennsylvania Academy of the Fine Arts is the first and oldest art museum and art school in the United States?
